Portal

In Portal, the player controls the protagonist, Chell, from a first-person perspective as she is challenged to navigate through a series of test chambers using the Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device, or portal gun, under the watchful supervision of the artificial intelligence GLaDOS. The portal gun can create two distinct portal ends, orange and blue which create a visual and physical connection between two different locations in three-dimensional space.

Many solutions exist for completing each puzzle, and sometimes you have to think on your feet! Another timeless classic.
