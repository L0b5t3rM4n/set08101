Starbound

Starbound is an open world sandbox game where you find yourself fleeing earth in a spaceship after a mysterious tentacle beast destroys it. As your spaceship crash-lands on a habitable Garden planet, an adventure begins that will take you hurtling across the universe.

Each time I beamed down from my personal starship into the 2D, 8-bit styled world, I found an experience that shoots for the stars and lands squarely among them. Exploration of a diverse array of planets, biomes, and subterranean nightmares is the highlight of the voyage, but the combat, bosses, and a rich backstory make it absolutely immersive.
